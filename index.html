Analyzing Toronto's Neighbourhoods

Insights for Toronto

  • Most bike commuters are downtown, but not in neighbourhoods with high bikeshare station density.
  • Bike parking racks are scarce in most neighbourhoods.
  • Bike theft rate is correlated with bikeshare station density in a neighbourhood.

Insights for Toronto vs Montreal

  • Toronto has a higher bikeshare station coverage (by percentage of city area) than Montreal's.

Main message: Toronto has sizable biking infrastructure, but it's ultimately lacking.
