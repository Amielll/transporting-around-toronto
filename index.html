    Now that we've done some of our exploring, let's see what the experts in the field are saying.
    According to People For Bikesâ€™ Bicycle Network Analysis (BNA) scoring system, Toronto ranks 262th out of 2390 North American cities. This is behind other major Canadian cities, Montreal with 71 and Vancouver with 66.
    BNA scores act as an indicator for how well a city's bike network connects people with places they want to go. This score is calculated based on 6 metrics, People, Opportunity, Core Services, Recreation, Retail, and Transit.
